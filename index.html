How It Works

You have the option of turning your vocal guide on immediately after registeration

You can initiate transfers and instant payment to other wallets and bank accounts.

You can make payment for your basic needs like electricity, DSTV subscription, etc.

Purchase airtime and data bundles for all the available networks in Nigeria

Authentication

To ensure wallet security, on opening the app, your identity is verified by asking you to fill in your password and mobile phone number which is compared with the database of the authorized user’s information.

Vocal guide

Immediately after registeration, you have an option to turn on the vocal feature which allows us guide you through the whole app interface and carry out necessary transactions. You can also switch between languages.

Achieve saving goals

You are able to fund your account and put away a certain percentage evertime you put money in your wallet.

See where your money goes

We help you track your spending and present the report to you via a simple and easy to read data sheet. This helps you keep a record of your transactions day in day out.
